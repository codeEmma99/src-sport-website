About the academy

Where campus sport turns into confidence, community, and competition.

Our story

Built for athletes who want more than a timetable.

Pentecost University SRC Sport Website brings coaching, fitness, events, competitive practice, and community engagement into one active space. Students can join structured programs, discover new sports, and build habits that carry into match day, class, work, and everyday life.

Every session is designed to feel practical: warm up properly, learn the skill, test it under pressure, reflect, and come back sharper.

Mission

How training works

A simple rhythm athletes can trust

  1. 01Assess

    Coaches identify skill level, confidence, fitness, and preferred sport.

  2. 02Train

    Sessions focus on technique, movement, decision-making, and team habits.

  3. 03Compete

    Players apply their work through fixtures, challenges, events, and friendly matches.

  4. 04Reflect

    Feedback helps athletes know exactly what to improve before the next block.
