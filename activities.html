Community Q&A

Ask about activities

What equipment is needed for taekwondo?

Comfortable training clothes are enough for the first session. Protective gear is introduced before sparring.

Can beginners join volleyball?

Yes. Beginner sessions focus on ball control, movement, and learning court positions.
